1. Our Belief

We believe everyone deserves 100% privacy. Dr.Clash is designed to collect only the information necessary to provide the Service while keeping your data as secure as reasonably possible. No tracking, no profiling, no data mining. Your data stays yours.

Dr.Clash is a fan-made Clash of Clans companion tool. We are not affiliated with Supercell.

2. What We Collect

Only the minimum information necessary for the Service to function. The app does not use analytics, crash reporting, or tracking technologies unless explicitly stated in this Privacy Policy. If this changes in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated before such technologies are used.

Dr.Clash does not collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, usage analytics, location data, or other personal information beyond what is described in this Privacy Policy. Third-party services, such as Google AdMob, may collect certain information in accordance with their own privacy policies.
