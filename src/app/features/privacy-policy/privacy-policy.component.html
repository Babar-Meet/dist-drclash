Privacy Policy

Effective: July 23, 2026

1. Our Belief

We believe in 100% privacy and 100% security. Dr.Clash is designed to collect nothing beyond what is strictly necessary for the app to function. No tracking, no profiling, no data mining. Your data stays yours.

Dr.Clash is a fan-made Clash of Clans companion tool. We are not affiliated with Supercell.

2. What We Collect

Absolutely nothing beyond the bare minimum. The app contains no analytics SDK, no crash reporting SDK, and no tracking code of any kind.

  • Account (Optional): If you sign up, Firebase Authentication stores your email and a hashed password. We also store your email and display name in Firestore for profile purposes. Core features work without an account.
  • Game Data (Your Choice): JSON data you import stays on your device. Subscribers can optionally sync it to the cloud to access across their own devices.
  • Player Tag & Preference: Saved locally on your device only.

We do not collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, usage analytics, location data, or any other personal information. Not even crash reports.

We are not responsible for how third parties handle your data. Please review their policies directly.

4. Data Storage

We only collect data for the greater good - to make the app work better for everyone.

  • Local: Your data is stored on your device. You control it. Clear app data or uninstall to remove everything.
  • Cloud: Only used by subscribers who choose to sync across their own devices. Your profile and exported upgrade data are stored there.
